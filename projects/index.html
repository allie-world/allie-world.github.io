Ask an Intern | Creative Producer

Senior Video Producer: Jason Jenkins
Intern content is notoriously stale. I pitched a reimagining of the format, drawing inspiration from high-fashion editorial interviews (think Vogue’s 73 Questions) to strip away the corporate veneer.
The goal was a format that felt delightfully watchable while quietly informative. I developed a narrative arc and custom questions designed to share key details about what makes algorithm development at HRT unique, without ever feeling like a lecture.
